Why the EduTrack System?

When comparing our capabilities and proposed solution to that of competitors, some benefits of choosing PaySwitch includes:

Enhancing Students’ Basic IT skills of – A Dual Learning Platform

The EduTrack system serves an opportunity to students of all calibre, to gain and build basic knowledge in IT within the course of their study.

Keeping Track of Students Studying During the Vacations

Teachers can examine and keep track of their students while they are away on vacations. Students can easily continue with studies when school resumes.

Increase Quality of Education and Academic Performance

With over 1000+ SHS students enrolled under the Ghana Double Track system, the EduTrack system promises a higher quality of education. Learning is also made simpler, easier and consistent.
